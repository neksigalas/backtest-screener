Backtest Screener simulates running a stock screener every trading day over a historical period, enters virtual positions when stocks match your criteria, and closes them when Take Profit / Stop Loss / Max Hold is hit.

1
Set Screener Filters
Choose RSI threshold, sector, consecutive red days, and other entry conditions in the left panel.
2
Configure Exit Rules
Set your Take Profit %, Stop Loss %, and Max Hold Days. These determine when each position closes.
3
Choose Period & Universe
Select how far back to test (3m / 6m / 1yr) and which stocks to scan (S&P 100, 500, or NASDAQ 100).
4
Analyze Results
Review Win Rate, EV per trade, P&L curve, and sector breakdown. Export all trades as CSV.
★
Options marked with ★ have been shown to improve performance in historical backtests. RSI ≤ 28 and ≥ 1 Consecutive Red Day are the top two filters by Expected Value.

Configure your strategy and run the simulation

Set your filters on the left, then click ▶ Run Backtest to see what results you would have gotten historically.
